Sheet Pan Chicken Gyros

Description

This sheet pan chicken gyros a quick lunch or summery dinner of spiced ground chicken, baked then broiled on a sheet pan. Serve on pita, and offer a selection of typical gyros toppings, like tomatoes, red onion, tzatziki sauce, feta cheese, cucumbers, and shredded lettuce.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground chicken
  • 3/4 cup fine dry breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • pita bread, for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat a 10x15-inch rimmed baking sheet with nonstick cooking spray. Move top oven rack to 6 inches below heat.
  2. Combine ground chicken, breadcrumbs, olive oil, egg, lemon zest, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, thyme, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper in a large bowl. Mix until well combined.
  3. Spread the chicken mixture evenly on the prepared baking sheet. Score the surface with a knife to create 12 rectangular portions, being careful not to cut all the way through.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 8 minutes. Switch oven to broiler and broil until the chicken is browned on top and cooked through, and an instant-read thermometer inserted into the center registers 165 degrees F (74 degrees C), 6 minutes.
  5. Serve warm with pita bread and desired toppings.
