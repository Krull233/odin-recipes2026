Tater Tot Breakfast Pizza

Description

This tater tot breakfast pizza is loaded with scrambled eggs, cheese, vegetables, sausage, bacon, and ham on a crispy tater tot crust.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 (30oz) package of tater tots
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 cup diced bell pepper, any color
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2/3 cup diced mushrooms
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 8 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1/2 cup crumbled, cooked breakfast sausage
  • 1/2 cup diced ham
  • 4 strips crumbled, cooked bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Spray a 12-inch oven-safe skillet with cooking spray, and add tater tot rounds, making sure to cover the bottom and come up the sides.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, heat half the butter in a nonstick skillet over medium heat. When the butter is sizzling, add bell pepper, onions, and mushrooms. Cook and stir frequently, until onions just begin to turn translucent, 2 to 3 minutes. Add spinach and cook until just wilted, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove vegetables from the pan.
  5. Add remaining butter to the same skillet over medium heat. When butter sizzles, add beaten eggs and cook until just set. They will cook more in the oven.
  6. Remove tater tots from the oven. Use a spatula or smaller pan to press down on tater tots to form an even layer, and to smash the tots together.
  7. Sprinkle 1 cup Cheddar cheese over tots, then layer in eggs; spread them evenly to edges of the skillet. Sprinkle with cooked vegetables, then add sausage. Sprinkle with ham, remaining 1 cup cheese, and finally add bacon.
  8. Return to the oven and bake until cheese is melted and pizza is heated through, about 10 minutes more.
